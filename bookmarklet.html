Add to Watchlist – Bookmarklet

On an IMDb title page, click the bookmarklet to add the title to your catalog (and your watchlist if signed in).

Setup:

  1. Drag the link below to your bookmarks bar
  2. Edit the bookmark and set watchlist-trailers.netlify.app to your site URL if different
Add to Watchlist

How it works:

  • Runs on IMDb title pages (e.g. imdb.com/title/tt1234567/)
  • Adds the title to your catalog (deduplicates if already there)
  • Opens your watchlist in a new tab to add to To Watch (you stay on IMDb)

Deploy: Set FIREBASE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT in Netlify env vars (base64 of serviceAccountKey.json): base64 -i serviceAccountKey.json | tr -d '\n'
